This is a small and simple calendar application with memo saving function.

Dots indicate dates with memos. To add a new memo press PLAY on the
date. Includes one off, annual, monthly, and weekly memos:

\begin{table}
  \begin{btnmap}{}{}
  PLAY & monthly \\
  LEFT & weekly \\
  RIGHT & annually \\
  ON & one off \\
  STOP & exit \\
  \end{btnmap}
\end{table}
