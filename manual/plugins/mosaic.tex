\subsection{Mosaic}

Press 
\opt{PLAYER_PAD}{\ButtonStop}
\opt{RECORDER_PAD,ONDIO_PAD,IRIVER_H100_PAD,IRIVER_H300_PAD}{\ButtonOff}
\opt{IPOD_4G_PAD,IPOD_3G_PAD}{\ButtonMenu}
\opt{IAUDIO_X5_PAD}{\ButtonPower}
to quit.
